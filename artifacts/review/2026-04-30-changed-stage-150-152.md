- **F10 agent_feedback_service.py match_rules set repr**: confirm, keep high.

### Systemic Observations (from arbitration)

The codebase shows a recurring pattern of missing timeouts on I/O boundaries (DB commits, readiness probes, CI steps) and insufficient concurrency control on shared mutable state (global asyncio locks, ORM read-modify-write cycles). These issues cluster around lifecycle edges — initialization, shutdown, and feedback paths — that receive less traffic than the hot request path. A single hardening pass adding `asyncio.wait_for` ceilings and `with_for_update` semantics to all DB mutations would eliminate most of the confirmed findings.
